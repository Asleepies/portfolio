How To Play:

- Each round your team will pick a Psychic. The psychic draws a card and looks at the random target area.
- They will give a one word clue about the location of the target area in relation to the drawn card, then your team will move the slider to where you think it is.
- After setting your guess, the other team will have a chance to guess if the target is to the left or right of your team's guess.
- The target is then revealed and points are awarded. First to 10 wins.
